# Formalization Roadmap

## Staged Lean plan

- **Level 1:** Pairwise winner theorem.
- **Level 2:** Cancellation lemma.
- **Level 3:** Finite antichain hit-at-most-once theorem.
- **Level 4:** Finite chain-certificate selection theorem.
- **Level 5:** Later work on deeper relations to NAG, hopfion data, and Erdős #1196.

## Quasi-autoformalization roles

- **Decomposer:** break theory statements into atomic lemmas/definitions.
- **Translator:** map mathematical prose into Lean-friendly formal statements.
- **Solver:** discharge local proof obligations.
- **Conductor:** orchestrate dependencies, proof order, and integration.

This roadmap is intentionally staged and conservative; early files are scaffold-level with `sorry` placeholders.
